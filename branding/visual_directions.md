# HeriStack — 5 Visual Identity Directions

## Direction 1: Technical Grid (SELECTED)
Colors: Navy #0A1628, Gold #C9A84C, White #FFFFFF
Typography: Courier New (monospace) for headings, clean sans-serif for body
Logo: Stacked layers with arch on top — representing heritage built in layers
Imagery: Code snippets, 3D outputs, before/after reconstructions
Tone: Confident builder, show real work

## Direction 2: Stone & Steel
Colors: Stone grey #8B8B83, Steel blue #4A6FA5, Cream #F5F0E8
Typography: Serif for heritage feel, bold sans for tech contrast
Logo: Chisel meeting circuit board
Imagery: Old walls next to digital overlays
Tone: Serious, academic, archival

## Direction 3: Warm Documentary
Colors: Warm brown #8B4513, Amber #FFBF00, Off-white #FAF0E6
Typography: Slab serif, humanist sans
Logo: Camera lens inside arch
Imagery: Field photos, people, places
Tone: Human, storytelling, inviting

## Direction 4: Minimal White Lab
Colors: Pure white #FFFFFF, Black #000000, Single accent #C9A84C
Typography: Ultra clean sans-serif only
Logo: Simple H + S lettermark
Imagery: Clean diagrams, wireframes, maps
Tone: Research lab, minimal, precise

## Direction 5: Night Archive
Colors: Deep purple #1A0A2E, Electric blue #00D4FF, White #FFFFFF
Typography: Futuristic mono font
Logo: Glowing arch outline
Imagery: Night photography, light trails, scans
Tone: Futuristic, dramatic, cutting edge

## Why Direction 1 was chosen
Matches our web engineering track, shows real technical work,
professional enough for mentors and externally shareable.
