# HeriStack — Branding Strategy

## Team Name
HeriStack

## Theme Selected
Future Heritage Studio — clean technical style, builder tone, 
process-focused content showing real work and outputs.

## Logo Direction
- Icon: stacked layers with a building/arch silhouette on top
- Style: minimal, flat, monospace feel
- Colors: deep navy (#0A1628), warm gold (#C9A84C), white (#FFFFFF)
- Typography: monospace for tech feel, clean sans-serif for body

## Target Audience
University students aged 18-24 in Pakistani cities who are 
interested in technology and want to contribute to something meaningful.
Secondary audience: heritage enthusiasts and educators aged 25-45.

## Platform Choice
LinkedIn organization page — professional, builder-focused, 
fits our technical audience and PMW mission.

## Tone
Confident, technical, mission-driven. We show our work — 
screenshots, code, outputs — not just talk about it.

## Why This Theme Fits HeriStack
We are a web engineering team building digital tools for 
heritage preservation. Future Heritage Studio fits because:
- We build real things (code, pages, tools)
- Our audience is technically curious
- We want to show process not just results
- Clean grid layouts match our web engineering background

## ICP Summary
Curious university students who grew up near historical places 
and want to use their coding skills for something that matters.
