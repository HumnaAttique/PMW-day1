# HeriStack — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Persona 1: The Curious University Student
- Age: 18-24
- City: Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad
- Motivation: Wants to learn how technology can solve real-world problems
- Pain points: Doesn't know how to contribute to heritage preservation
- Online behavior: Active on Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube
- Why they care: They grew up near historical places and want to see them preserved

## Persona 2: The Heritage Enthusiast
- Age: 25-35
- City: Any Pakistani city with historical sites
- Motivation: Passionate about history and culture
- Pain points: Sees historical sites deteriorating with no digital backup
- Online behavior: Follows history pages, reads articles, shares cultural content
- Why they care: Believes technology should protect what history left behind

## Persona 3: The Tech-Curious Educator
- Age: 30-45
- City: Urban Pakistan
- Motivation: Wants to show students that coding has real purpose
- Pain points: Lacks tools to make heritage education engaging
- Online behavior: LinkedIn, educational YouTube channels
- Why they care: Sees HeriStack as a bridge between technology and culture

## Target Platform: LinkedIn + GitHub Pages
## Tone: Professional, builder-focused, mission-driven
