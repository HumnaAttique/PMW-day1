HeriStack is a web engineering team using modern technology to digitally preserve Pakistan's cultural heritage sites before they are lost forever.

View Our Work on GitHub

What We're Building

3D Reconstruction

Using monocular depth estimation and AI models to create digital 3D previews of heritage sites from photos.

Web Tools

Building accessible web interfaces that let anyone explore and contribute to heritage preservation efforts.

Open Research

Documenting and sharing our findings on COLMAP, NeRF, Gaussian Splatting, and depth estimation methods.

Our Team

HeriStack is part of the PreserveMy.World internship program. We are software engineering students from FAST-NUCES Islamabad building real tools for a real mission.
